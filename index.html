About Us

The ChavanNelson Foundation CNF is a registered non-profit organization that designs and executes programmes and projects to reduce the socioeconomic difficulties of children/youth and the less fortunate. As a result, and for a greater impact, the ChavanNelson Foundation CNF maintains and functions under the three pillars: Education, Youth and Welfare.

As a charity foundation that is christian-principled, CNF has a strong belief that 'by touching one life at a time, (together) we change our future'. The foundation was instituted as Gift of Change (GOC), April 2012, and since then, it has gone through great development in order to integrate different focus areas and widen its global possibilities.

Vision

Our vision is to mobile motivated local and global citizens who are committed to impacting Education, Youth and Welfare through charity.

Mission

Our mission is to promote benevolent activities that would seek to encourage giving time, resources and self to better the circumstances of our nation's most vulnerable.
